For 50 years, Pima Community College has been changing the lives of individuals and communities through the promise of education. This work often is made possible through the generous support of our independent and enterprise partners.


As a driver of economic development in the region, Pima must stay ahead of trends and be nimble enough to adapt to change. Partnerships with employers, civic groups and philanthropic organizations allow the College to meet the needs of students and the community. Investments in Pima allow the College to provide leading-edge programs, equipment and services, so that students are prepared for the jobs of today and tomorrow.


In our growing metropolitan area, the need for highly skilled, better-educated employees is greater than ever. Equally important, these opportunities allow graduates to thrive and grow right here in Pima County. Pima's Centers of Excellence are hubs for innovation, bringing together students, employers, and other partners.


Pima Community College Foundation Inc (Pima Foundation) was founded to provide much-needed support to the College and its students.


Your gift will help students directly through scholarships, equipment and other support for programs, academic support services, and cultural and global opportunities.


Please contact Pima Foundation to learn how your generosity contributes to the academic excellence of our students and to the vitality of our College and community.
